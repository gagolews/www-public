Research

Research Interests

My current research interests are related to data science (with particular emphasis on modelling of complex phenomena, developing of usable, general purpose algorithms, studying analytical properties thereof, and finding out how people use, misuse, understand, and misunderstand methods of data analysis in research, commercial, and decision making settings).

These include:

  • computational and applied statistics,

  • statistical and data analysis software,

  • machine learning and data mining algorithms,

  • complex data aggregation and fusion, prototype learning, clustering,

  • *metrics,

  • decision making,

  • complex systems,

  • mathematical modelling in sports, social sciences, economics, science of science (including bibliometrics and informetrics), and meta-research in general.

If you feel like we should write a paper together on something interesting and/or fun, drop me an email.

For more details, see my list of Publications as well as my CV.

Prospective Research Students

I am eligible for supervising research students at the PhD level. I was the principal supervisor of 3 PhD and 11 MSc by research students from commencement through to successful completion.

Feel welcome to email me if you have any interesting research ideas as well as the essential skills such as in programming (e.g., Python, R, C/C++), data wrangling, matrix algebra, probability and statistics, and optimisation.

But – please – read a few of my papers first. I am not responding to emails that look like spam (ones that seem to be sent to many people at the same time).
